QUANTUM KERNELS PLAY DOGS VS CATS

 

This app has been developed to support the QuantumKernelsPlayDogsVSCats project.

Among its functionalities are:

  • Performing classification of dog or cat images entered by the user. For this prediction a model based on convolutional neural networks will be used with different variants such as Transfer Learning with Feature Extraction, Support Vector Machines, Quantum Kernels or Variational Quantum Circuits.
  • Training of a model applying Transfer Learning in which the user can choose the different parameters to be used for training.
  • Consult a history with the details of the models already trained and previous trainings.
